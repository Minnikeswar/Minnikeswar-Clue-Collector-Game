Clue Collector

Solve the Sudoku

To play the game, first choose a digit from the bottom row of the board. Once you have selected a digit, click on the empty block in the grid where you would like to place it. If the selection is correct, the digit will be placed in the block. If the selection is incorrect, the error count will increase.

Keep in mind that each digit can only appear once in each row, column, and block of nine squares. So, as you progress through the game, be sure to check that your selections are valid and don't violate any of these rules.
