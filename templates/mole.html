In this game, you'll see moles and cacti popping up in random locations on the screen. Your objective is to click on the moles and avoid the cacti.

To play the game, simply click on the moles as soon as you see them appear on the screen. Remember to avoid the cacti! Your score will be displayed at the top of the screen, and will increase by 5 points every time you click on a mole, or decrease by 5 points if you accidentally click on a cactus.

The game will continue until you reach a score of 75!
